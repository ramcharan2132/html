Welcome to learn about "Satoru Gojo"

The strongest sorcerer in the history of mankind...

  • Full Name: Satoru Gojo
  • Occupation: Jujutsu Sorcerer, Teacher at Jujutsu High
  • Affiliation: Jujutsu High, Tokyo Metropolitan Magic Technical College
  • Abilities: Limitless, Six Eyes, Domain Expansion (Unlimited Void)
  • Personality: Confident, Playful, Overpowered, Protective, Unconventional

Legacy:

Gojo's presence in Jujutsu Kaisen is pivotal for both his students and the overall plot. His overwhelming power, combined with his distinctive personality and goals, set him apart as one of the series' most memorable characters. While his strength makes him a near-untouchable figure, his relationships with those around him, especially his students, provide a deeper layer to his character.
