Legacy:

Itachi Uchiha remains one of the most well-regarded and tragic characters in the Naruto series. His internal struggle between his love for his family, his loyalty to his village, and the overwhelming burden of his choices create a deeply compelling character arc. Itachi embodies themes of sacrifice, duty, and the heavy cost of war, and his character resonates with fans because of the depth and nuance in his actions.
